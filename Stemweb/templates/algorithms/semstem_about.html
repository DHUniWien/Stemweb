Semstem is based on a structural expectation-maximization (structural EM) algorithm. It is the first computer-based method able to estimate general latent tree structures, unlike earlier methods that are usually restricted to bifurcating trees where all the extant texts are placed in the leaf nodes.

Related Paper: T. Roos and Y. Zou, (2011). Analysis of Textual Variation by Latent Tree Structures, to appear in Proc. ICDM-2011, Vancouver.
